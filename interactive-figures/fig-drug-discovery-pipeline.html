PARK2 in Cancer
PARK2 downregulation across multiple tumor types links Parkin biology to oncology.
Model
iPSC-DA Neurons
9 donor lines. TH⁺/GIRK2⁺ dopaminergic identity. 4–6 wk maturation. MEA-validated.
Model
Dual-Hit Protocol
PFF + IFN-γ to model neuroinflammatory Lewy body pathology. 21-day time course.
Model
CRISPR KO Lines
Parkin KO, GBA KO, α-syn KO, LRRK2 KO iPSCs for genetic disease modeling.
Cardiac
iPSC-Cardiomyocytes
GiWi differentiation. >90% cTnT⁺. Cardiotoxicity & safety pharmacology.
Screening
High-Content Imaging
96/384-well confocal with automated segmentation. Inclusion count, mitophagy, viability.
Screening
FLIPR & Incucyte
Kinetic calcium flux and real-time proliferation / cytotoxicity monitoring.
Screening
Plate-Based Readouts
CellTiter-Glo, LDH, caspase-3/7, ROS (CellROX), SA-β-gal for senescence.
MoA
Western Blot PD Panel
pS129 α-syn, LAMP1/2, TFEB, NRF2, HDAC6, LC3B, p62 — multi-readout PD confirmation.
MoA
Pseudovirus Neutralization
GFP-pseudotyped virus entry assay. Dose-response inhibition of Spike-mediated entry.
MoA
TMT Proteomics
Subcellular fractionation + TMT-MS to map drug-induced proteome shifts.
Biomarker
TE/PD Decision Matrix
Target engagement (Parkin activity) + pharmacodynamic (inclusion clearance) biomarker panel.
Biomarker
Cytokine Profiling
MSD V-PLEX multiplex for IL-6, TNF-α, IFN-γ, IL-1β — inflammatory response readouts.
Output
