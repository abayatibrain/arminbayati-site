CRISPR/Cas9 Workflow Detail
gR
Guide RNA Design & Synthesis
Computational design targeting exonic regions. Off-target scoring via Benchling/CRISPOR. Synthetic sgRNAs or U6-driven expression vectors.
RNP
Cas9-RNP Assembly & Nucleofection
Pre-complexed Cas9 + sgRNA ribonucleoprotein. Lonza 4D nucleofection into iPSCs. Minimizes off-target via transient delivery.
QC
Clone Selection & Validation
Single-cell expansion → PCR genotyping → Sanger sequencing. Western blot confirms protein loss. Karyotype + pluripotency ICC (Oct4, Sox2, Nanog, TRA-1-60).
Δ
Functional Characterization
Differentiate to disease-relevant lineage. Assess phenotype vs. isogenic controls. Multi-readout comparison: ICC, WB, live-cell, proteomics.
