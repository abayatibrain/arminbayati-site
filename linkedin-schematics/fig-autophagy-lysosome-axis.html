Autophagy-Lysosome Degradation Axis

Macroautophagy pathway from initiation through lysosomal degradation and nutrient recycling

Pathway Stages
Initiation — ULK1 complex activation
Nucleation — PI3KC3-mediated PIP3 production
Expansion — ATG5-12-16L & LC3 lipidation
Autophagosome — Sealed double-membrane vesicle
Autolysosome — Fusion & active degradation
Regulatory — mTOR, AMPK, TFEB signaling
Key Regulatory Proteins
AMPK: Phosphorylates ULK1 upon energy stress
mTORC1: Phosphorylates & inhibits ULK1 when nutrients abundant
Beclin1: Scaffolds PI3KC3 complex nucleation
LC3: Ubiquitin-like autophagy marker & SNARE interaction
TFEB: Master regulator of lysosomal biogenesis genes
p62/SQSTM1: Selective cargo receptor for ubiquitinated substrates
