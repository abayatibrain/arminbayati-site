Exosome-Mediated Cell-to-Cell Propagation

Pathological protein transfer via extracellular vesicles in neurodegeneration

Exosomal transfer of misfolded proteins is a key mechanism in prion-like propagation of neurodegenerative diseases. GW4869 blocks nSMase2-dependent exosome biogenesis; alternative routes include tunneling nanotubes (TNTs) for direct cytoplasmic continuity.
